Top 5 Tips for Night-time Loc Care

Your night-time routine determines the health and longevity of your locs. Here are our top 5 professional tips:

1. Use a Satin or Silk Bonnet

Cotton pillowcases absorb moisture from your locs and create friction. A satin scarf or bonnet keeps your natural oils where they belong—in your hair.

2. Avoid Sleeping on Damp Locs

Going to bed with wet locs can lead to "loc rot" or a musty smell. Ensure your locs are 100% dry before laying down.

3. Light Scalp Massage

Use a few drops of our Essential Growth Oil to massage your scalp for 2 minutes. This stimulates blood flow and promotes growth while you rest.

4. Loose Styles Only

If you tie your locs up, keep the scrunchie loose. Tight styles at night put unnecessary tension on your edges and roots.

5. Check for Lint

Lint is the enemy of clean locs! Keeping your hair covered at night is the #1 way to prevent lint from bedding from getting trapped in your hair fibers.
